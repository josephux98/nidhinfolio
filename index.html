My Story

My Story

I’m a UI UX designer from India currently working with Equinix team. Before this, I worked on Edgy, solving complex design problems. I graduated with a master’s in digital-media communication from Lindenwood University and hold a bachelors in Multi-Media Design

As a UX UI Designer, I strongly believe that a perfect user interface should look good and perform even better. Alongside clients, I undercover problems and solve them. Over the 3 years of my career, my portfolio includes user interface design, identity design, illustration and creative division. I have been doing small and big projects all these past years and saw how design thinking can solve real-world challenges at scale. In my journey in design, I’ve had the opportunity to work in both startup and enterprise environments on projects related to education, mobility, and health care.

Coming from a background in both traditional and digital design, I love connecting the dots between business goals, technical constraints and user satisfaction. In my free time, I enjoy coming of age stories, exploring new cuisines, and looking for last minute adventures.
